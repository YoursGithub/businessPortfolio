Basic Institutions and Elements for Business

RUSSIA

  • 1. Taxpayer Identification Number (INN)
    Mandatory for all businesses to conduct tax-related activities in Russia. The Federal Tax Service issues it during the business registration process. Federal Tax Service
  • 2. Certificate of State Registration (OGRN)
    Issued by the Federal Tax Service, confirms the state registration of a business entity. Required during business incorporation with identification information of founders and directors. Federal Tax Service
  • 3. Charter Documents
    Define the company's internal governance, structure, and scope of operations. Equivalent to foundational agreements or bylaws. Federal Tax Service
  • 4. Legal Address Proof
    Federal Notarial Chamber offers a notary search tool to find legal professionals performing verification of documents and identities. Federal Notarial Chamber
  • 5. Corporate Bank Account Statement
    Businesses must open a corporate bank account in Russia for financial transactions. Required documents include proof of registration, INN, and charter documents. Sberbank Russia
  • 6. Certificate of Tax Registration
    Following registration, businesses must register separately for tax payments to receive tax certificates and ensure compliance with local and federal tax laws. Federal Tax Service
  • 7. Company Seal
    A legal seal is required to authoritatively sign certain business documents. This is procured after receiving the state registration certificate.
  • 8. Licenses and Permits
    Specific industries require additional licenses from respective government bodies. Details available through Russia's Ministry of Economic Development. Ministry of Economic Development
  • 9. Public Services Portal (Gosuslugi)
    Provides access to various government services, including guidance on director verification. Gosuslugi
  • 10. Russian Post (Pochta Rossii)
    Serves as authorized points for document submission or identity verification under certain business setups. Russian Post
  • 11. Social Security and Pension Fund Registration
    Employers need to register with social security and pension authorities to make mandatory contributions. Pension Fund of Russia

Public/Government-Supported Incubation Centers

Skolkovo Innovation Center

Supported by the Russian government, fostering innovation through incubation programs for startups across sectors such as IT, energy, and biotechnology. Offers R&D support, mentorship, and infrastructure.

Bortnik Foundation (FASIE)

State-funded foundation providing startup grants and technical assistance for innovative businesses in Russia. Supports technology commercialization.

Ingria Business Incubator

Partially public-supported incubation center in St. Petersburg helping startups with resources like mentorship, investment access, and business infrastructure.

INDIA

  • 1. Permanent Account Number (PAN)
    A unique identifier issued by the Income Tax Department essential for opening a bank account, filing tax returns, and other financial activities. NSDL e-Gov
  • 2. GST Registration
    Mandatory for businesses with annual turnovers exceeding â‚¹20 Lakhs, required to collect and remit GST. GST Portal
  • 3. Certificate of Incorporation
    Issued by the Registrar of Companies (RoC), legally recognizes a business as a corporate entity. Apply through SPICe form. Ministry of Corporate Affairs
  • 4. MoA and AoA
    Memorandum of Association and Articles of Association define the company's purpose, duties, internal rules, and operations. Ministry of Corporate Affairs
  • 5. Director Identification Number (DIN)
    Mandatory for anyone intending to become a director in a company in India. Ministry of Corporate Affairs - DIN Application
  • 6. Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
    Required for filing electronic forms with government departments such as MCA. MCA Digital Certificate Providers
  • 7. Shops and Establishment Registration
    Mandatory for businesses operating in commercial spaces. Registration is done with the local municipal corporation.
  • 8. Professional Tax Registration
    Applicable to businesses employing staff, mandated by most state governments. Maharashtra Professional Tax
  • 9. Trade License
    Required from the municipal corporation to operate legally. Online Services of MCD
  • 10. FSSAI License
    Mandatory for businesses engaged in food-related operations. FSSAI Portal
  • 11. Import Export Code (IEC)
    Mandatory for businesses involved in import or export. DGFT IEC Application
  • 12. Udyam Registration
    For Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to avail benefits under government schemes. Udyam Registration
  • 13. Employee State Insurance (ESI) Registration
    Mandatory for businesses hiring more than 10 employees. ESIC Portal
  • 14. Provident Fund (PF) Registration
    Required for organizations with 20 or more employees to ensure financial security for workers. Employees' Provident Fund Organisation
